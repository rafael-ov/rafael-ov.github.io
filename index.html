Let me introduce myself.

I'm 25 years old, and I've been a freelance designer for 5 years, fascinated by new technologies and by learning and correlating possibly different areas of knowledge!

Profile

Designer and Videomaker since 2017, whose main area of expertise is Video Editing, Capture, and Motion Graphics. In July 2018, I joined as a Product Design intern at Microduino, a multinational company in the field of educational technology based in China, Brazil, and the United States, and 2019 I was appointed Product Designer, working on the creation of projects and scripting, recording and editing of video lessons, institutional and product videos. Currently, working as a freelance designer working mainly in the areas of Graphic Design, 3D modeling and printing, and Videomaker.
